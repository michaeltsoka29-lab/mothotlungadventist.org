Frequently Asked Questions

What time are your services?
Our main worship service (Sabbath School) begins at 09:00 AM every Saturday, followed by the Divine Service at 11:00 AM. We sometimes have mid-week prayer meetings on Wednesdays.
Where is the church located?
We are located in Mothotlung, Disteneng Katakane Street, Mothutlung Rd, Brits, 0268, South Africa. You can find a detailed map and directions in the "Visit Us" section at the bottom of our home page.
What should I expect when I visit?
You can expect a warm welcome, heartfelt congregational singing, a focus on Bible study, and a Christ-centered sermon. We are a family-friendly community with programs for all ages.
Do I need to be a member to attend?
No. Everyone is welcome to join our services, regardless of their religious background or membership status. We would be honored to have you as our guest.
How can I get involved in community outreach?
We have various outreach programs including food distribution and health expos. You can visit our Community Outreach page or fill out the contact form to volunteer.
Who can I contact for prayer?
You can reach our Prayer Line directly at +27 83 568 1837 or use the contact form on our website to submit a prayer request.
